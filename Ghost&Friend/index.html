Ghost & Friend

  • Originality: This is a co-op action/puzzle game with a ghost, and also a not-ghost!
  • Technical Merit: This is the first time I've built a game where two different protagonists can be controlled simultaneously.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I'm still figuring out how, exactly, to get basic stuff to work in Phaser. Having music play was surprisingly difficult.
  • Prototype Assets: I made most of the assets from screatch. However, I borrowed the sky_bg from the tutorial and the music from Phaser examples.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Just, other top-down games like classic Zelda, or Pac-Man.
  • High Concept: A ghost and their friend go on adventures together, to collect glowy things! They need to co-operate; the ghost can pass through some barriers, while only the ghost's friend can interact with levers and stuff. Together, they can do anything!
  • Theme: State which of the Storymatic themes you used.
  • Mandated Variety: No randomness; just bird's-eye movement and interaction.
  • Prototype Goal: Co-op play.
  • Player Experience Goals: I would like them to have fun, and to experience the friendship between a ghost and a person who is not a ghost.
  • Gameplay: You can move Ghost with WASD, and Friend with the Arrow Keys. Move in four directions, dodge obstacles and collect glowy things!
  • Strategies: You gotta co-ordinate and work together. Take it easy, though, unless you're doing a speedrun!
  • Story/Setting/Premise: see High Concept
  • Target Audience: Anyone!!
  • Play Time: Like, two minutes, maybe.
